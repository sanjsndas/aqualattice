Frequently Asked Questions

What are your customer service hours?

Our main customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9am to 6pm EST. Technical support has extended hours Monday-Friday 8am-8pm EST and Saturday 9am-5pm EST. Emergency support is available 24/7 for critical system failures that could lead to property damage or significant water loss.

How quickly can I expect a response to my email inquiry?

We strive to respond to all email inquiries within 24 business hours. Most emails received during business hours receive a response within 4-6 hours. Technical support emails typically receive a response within 2 business hours during support hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our support line for immediate assistance.

Do you offer on-site consultations?

Yes, we offer complimentary on-site consultations for projects within our service areas. Our design specialists will visit your location to assess your space, discuss your needs, and provide recommendations. Consultations typically last 60-90 minutes and include a follow-up proposal. Appointments can be scheduled through our installation department at (212) 555-4567 or install@aqualattice.com.

What information should I have ready when contacting support?

For fastest service, please have your system ID (found on your installation paperwork or equipment), a description of the issue including when it started, any error messages displayed, and steps you've already tried to resolve the problem. Photos or videos of the issue can be extremely helpful for our technicians to diagnose problems remotely.

How do I schedule maintenance services?

Maintenance services can be scheduled by calling (212) 555-1234 or emailing maintenance@aqualattice.com. We recommend seasonal maintenance visits in spring (system startup) and fall (winterization). Customers with maintenance contracts receive priority scheduling and discounted rates. Our online portal also allows you to view available appointments and schedule service directly.
